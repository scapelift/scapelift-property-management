## Secure completion checklist

- [ ] Store credentials only in the client's encrypted password manager or approved secure handoff system.
- [ ] Confirm the client controls the company email and its recovery options.
- [ ] Confirm at least two approved client administrators can access GitHub and Netlify.
- [ ] Confirm the client controls the registrar account and renewal billing.
- [ ] Confirm 2FA is enabled on GitHub, Netlify, the registrar, DNS provider, and company email.
- [ ] Store recovery codes separately from account passwords.
- [ ] Remove former developers and revoke obsolete tokens, sessions, deploy keys, and application authorizations.
- [ ] Test recovery procedures without exposing secrets in tickets, chat, email, or Git.
